Our Claim Settlement Process

Filing an insurance claim can be stressful, but our dedicated team ensures the process is smooth, transparent, and hassle-free. We assist you at every step—from documentation to final payout.

1

Intimation

Notify us immediately about the incident (hospitalization, accident, or loss) to initiate the claim process.

2

Documentation

Submit the required documents. We'll provide you with a detailed checklist to ensure nothing is missed.

3

Verification

The insurer reviews your submitted documents and claim form. A surveyor may be appointed if necessary.

4

Settlement

Once approved, the claim amount is settled directly to the hospital (cashless) or reimbursed to your bank account.
