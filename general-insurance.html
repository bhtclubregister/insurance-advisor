General Insurance

Protect your valuable assets including your home, car, and business against unforeseen risks.

What is General Insurance?

General insurance covers everything other than your life. Whether it is a motor vehicle, a commercial property, a residential home, or travel, general insurance products protect the things you value against fire, theft, natural disasters, and third-party liabilities.

Types of General Insurance

Motor Insurance

Mandatory protection for your car or two-wheeler against accidents, theft, and third-party liabilities.

Home Insurance

Safeguard your most precious asset against fire, burglary, floods, and earthquakes.

Travel Insurance

Stay protected against medical emergencies, flight cancellations, and lost baggage while traveling abroad.

Commercial Insurance

Protect your business premises, inventory, and employees from unexpected commercial risks.
